Ingredients

2 Eggs 1 cup of Butter 1 teaspoon Baking powder 2 teaspoons Vanilla extract 1 cup of White sugar 1 cup of Brown sugar 3 cups of Flour 2 cups of Chocolate chips

Recipe

Home
  • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Give it enough time to fully heat so your cookies bake evenly. Meanwhile, line three baking sheets with parchment paper and set them aside.
    		•
  • In a medium bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Whisk or stir until everything is evenly mixed, then set this bowl aside.
    		•
  • In a large bowl, add the butter along with both sugars. Cream them together using a mixer or spoon until the mixture is smooth, well combined, and slightly fluffy.
    		•
  • Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Then add the vanilla extract and beat the mixture for about 1 minute, or until it looks light and slightly airy.
    		•
  • Gradually add the dry ingredients into the wet mixture. Mix gently until everything is just combined, being careful not to overmix.
    		•
  • Stir in the chocolate chips, making sure they are evenly distributed throughout the dough. Scoop out about 2–3 tablespoons of dough at a time. Roll each portion into a ball and place them on the prepared baking sheets, leaving space between each one.
    		•
  • Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 8–10 minutes. Remove them when the edges are just starting to turn lightly golden. Let the cookies sit on the baking sheet for 5 minutes to set.
    		•
  • Transfer the cookies to a cooling rack and allow them to cool completely before serving or storing.
